Live Music Association



LMA is one of Northeastern's music industry clubs.
I joined their design team to help make posters
to promote artists playing on campus along with making posts
for several social media pages.



The project I am most proud of from this club so far
is this poster I made to promote a concert featuring the
bands Day Wave and Animal Flag. After learning how to make
vector portraits in Illustrator in 2D Tools, I wanted to
digitally draw Jackson Phillips from Day Wave for this poster.
